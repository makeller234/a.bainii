A. bainii

Aulacephalodon bainii live about 299-252 million years ago, during the Permian period. Aulacephalodon are a part of the dicynodontia taxon. Dicynodonts are characterized by having 2 tusks.

As you can tell from this clearly not photoshopped image of the A.bainii with my animals, it'd make a great household pet. That's what made me most interested in finding out more about it.

Well actually, the real reason I was intrigued by A.bainii is that I remembered that only 30% of terrestrial species survived the Permian extinction and I was curious to learn more about those species, and that led me to finding the A. bainii.
